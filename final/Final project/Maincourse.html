Maincourse of the Day

Butter Chicken

Ingredients: 3 pieces of boneless chicken, salt and red chilli to taste, and one teaspoon of; turmeric, cumin, blackpepper, large cardomum, cinnamon, clove, ginger (powder or freshly grated), star anised, 2-3 tablesppons lemon juice, tomato ketchup (2 tablespoons), half a can tomato sauce, half a cup of milk, half a cup fresh cream, quarter cup yoghurt (optional), half a stick of butter and freshly chopped cilantro

Instructions: Marinade chicken for 30 minutes with all the spices and lemon juice. Heat oil and add marinaded chicken and stir fry for 5 minutes. Then add the tomato sauce and milk and cook for 15 minutes. Stir in fresh cream, yoghurt,butter and tomato ketchup; cook for 2-3 minutes. Garnish with cilantro and serve!
