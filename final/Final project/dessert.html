Dessert of the Day

Cupcakes

Ingredients: cake mix, sprite (soda), coloring and icing to decorate

Instructions: Take the cake mix and slowly add enough sprite until you get a consistent texture which isn't too thin or too thick. Then place batter in a non-stick cupcake holder and bake for 30 minutes at 300 degrees. Insert a toothpick in the middle of the cupcake to check if baked, if toothpick comes out clean then take out the cupcakes, let them cook and then decorate with icing.
