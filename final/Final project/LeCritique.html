Le Critique

I recently went to Peking Express (grandriver, EL, MI) over thanksgiving break with a friend and be rest assured, it will be the last time I ever enter that establishment!

I am not complaining about the quality of the food, it was above average. I am complaining about the extremely horrible service.

The place was more than two-thirds empty, and the server would not let us sit in the table we wanted to. She just point-blank refused! And she never bothered bringing any of the additional items we requested like hot sauce, my friend had to go to the reception himself to get it. At times like these, I miss Dubai.
